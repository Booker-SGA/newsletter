SENIOR CLASS

Senior SGA is up and running! We are working hard at making sure this year will be the best senior year. Right now we are preparing for the year by coming up with fundraising ideas and trying to bring some more school spirit! A 'Senior Sunrise' is in the works and we will be sending out more information about senior T-shirts in the next week! Students will get to vote on the Tshirt and then we start selling!!! This senior year will be full of memories and surprises.

JUNIOR CLASS

The class of 2027 began the year decorating the Junior hallway to welcome back students. We also installed a legendary class board featuring the Junior SGA members. Currently we are working on deciding the homecoming theme and Spirt Week days. We are looking forward to homecoming to celebrate our team and show our school spirit!

SOPHOMORE CLASS

The Sophomore class brought the hallways to life with their class color - white - showcasing creativity, teamwork, and pride! Their section now gleams with spirit and excitement as they gear up to present their Homecoming theme for deliberation.

STUDENT BODY

We are excited to launch our newest initiative: Club Rush! Once a month during lunch, we spotlight a different club with fun activities. This exciting event boosts school spirit, sparks interest in clubs, and creates amazing social connections across our campus. Meanwhile, we're hard at work planning an unforgettable Homecoming dance. After hearing your ideas at our Homecoming mixer, we are maintaining momentum to create a truly collaborative Homecoming experience that brings our entire Booker community together!
